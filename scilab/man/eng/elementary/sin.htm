sin - sine function

Calling Sequence

[t]=sin(x)

Parameters

  • x: real or complex vector or matrix

Description

For a vector or a matrix, sin(x) is the sine of its elements. For matrix sine use sinm(X) function.

Examples


asin(sin([1,0,%i]))
